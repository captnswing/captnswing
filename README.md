## Hi, I'm Frank 👋

**Physicist turned engineering leader**, based in Stockholm 🇸🇪. I started out
modelling the visual cortex in theoretical physics, and have spent the last
~25 years building and leading engineering teams — across broadcast, streaming,
global music, and mobility platforms.

Today I lead **data engineering & data infrastructure** at Arrive (EasyPark Group).

  reduction end to end.
